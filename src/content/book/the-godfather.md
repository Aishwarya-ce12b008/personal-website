The epic tale of the Corleone crime family and their patriarch Don Vito Corleone, exploring power, loyalty, and the American Dream's dark side.

The book that defined the modern mafia genre. Puzo's writing is gripping, and the characters are unforgettable. It goes deeper than the movie, exploring the psychology of power and family loyalty.
